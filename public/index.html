Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get my Instagram sessionid?

Open Instagram in your browser → press F12 → go to Application tab → Cookies → instagram.com → find "sessionid" and copy its value.

Is my session safe?

Yes! Your session cookie is stored only in the server's temporary memory (RAM) and is automatically cleared when the server restarts. It is never saved to disk or shared with anyone.

Why can't I download from private profiles?

Private profile content requires authentication. Use the "Private & Exclusive" tab with your session ID to access content from private accounts you follow.

What formats are supported?

Videos are downloaded in MP4 format, and images in their original format (usually JPEG). We always fetch the highest quality available.
